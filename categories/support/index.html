When is a bug not a bug? When it’s a “design decision”

Last month I wrote about a problem that Informatica as part of OBIA was causing us, wherein an expired database account would bring Oracle down by virtue of multiple connections from Informatica. I raised an SR with Oracle (under OBIA support), who after some back-and-forth with Informatica, were told: This is not a bug. That the two error messages coming back from Oracle are handled differently is the result of a design decision and as such not a product fault.
